How it Works

Register

Register with your Google account. You can register as an at-risk population member seeking assistance, or as an individual willing to volunteer to assist local at-risk individuals.

Choose Locations

Select up to 5 local stores that you frequent in your neighborhood where you buy food and other essential supplies.

Connect

We'll give you the option to connect with other registered users, who are in close geographic proximity to you, and who also frequent the same stores as you.
