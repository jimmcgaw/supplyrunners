About

This site was created with a singular mission: to facilitate connecting at-risk members of the population, who must remain at home, with other residents in close geographic proximity to them who are willing to purchase food or other essential supplies for them.

While there are many organizations and charities that are designed to provide support in this regard, this site is intended to be peer-to-peer and directly connect people where these options are not available or have been overwhelmed due to the sudden surge in demand.

The following is some more information in a question / answer format:

Do I exchange money with others on this site?

No. This site is intended to connect people, not act as a payment broker. We expect you to coordinate any financial arrangements with each other.

Do you store or expose my home address or current location?

No. The website may ask for your current location or use your IP address, but only for the purpose of helping you find your local stores. We do not store this information, nor do we expose it to others.

Do I communicate with other users through this site?

No. You can opt to be contacted via your email address or via a phone number that you provide. When you make yourself visible to registered users on this site, those in your neighborhood can reach out to you via the contact means you specify.
