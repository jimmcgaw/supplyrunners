Connecting At-Risk Individuals with Local Residents

This website was created in response to the shelter-in-place order issued in the United States to combat the spread of the coronavirus and COVID-19.

The mission? Connect at-risk members of the population with local residents who frequent the same shops and stores, so residents can purchase food for them.

Who's at risk?

You can register as either:

  1. an at-risk member of the population seeking assistance, OR
  2. a volunteer willing to pick up food and supplies for a nearby at-risk resident.
